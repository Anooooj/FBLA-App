What is FBLA

FBLA is the high school division of Future Business Leaders of America-Phi Beta Lambda, Inc. FBLA helps high school students prepare for careers in business through academic competitions (FBLA Competitive Events), leadership development, and educational programs.

What types of events are there?

There are two ways to break down events, by size and by category.

There are three sizes: Individual, Team, and Chapter. Individual events involve only one person, Team events involve multiple people from the school chapter, and Chapter event involve multiple people from the local chapter, which include multiple schools.

There are four categories: Test, Presentation, Interview, and Team Performance. Tests involve taking an exam, Presentations involve presenting before a panel of judges, Interviews involve an interview, and Team Performance involves working with a team.
